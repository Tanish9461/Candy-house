What Makes Werther’s Original Special?

Much has changed since Werther’s Original Hard Candies were first created. But some things have remained exactly the same. Our candies are made with only the finest ingredients, and blended together with the same passion and care our first candy maker, Gustav Nebel, brought to every caramel he crafted. That’s how we make sure every Werther’s Original has the familiar taste you love.

About Us

Today’s Werther’s Original The irresistible caramel hard candy created by Gustav Nebel over 100 years ago is still, to this day, one of the most popular and best selling caramel candies in the world. In fact, people in over 100 countries consider it a favorite, making Werther’s Original the most international of the Storck family of brands. In 2010, the Karamell-Küche presented by Werther's Original in Epcot® at the Walt Disney World® Resort was established. Luckily, some things never change – each caramel creation is made with loving care and proudly bears the Werther’s Original name.
